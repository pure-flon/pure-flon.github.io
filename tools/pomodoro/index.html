About the Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo. It uses a timer to break work into focused intervals (traditionally 25 minutes), separated by short breaks.

  • Work session: Focus on one task for 25 minutes without interruptions.
  • Short break: Take a 5-minute break to rest your mind.
  • Long break: After 4 work sessions, take a longer 15-minute break.
  • Customizable: Adjust all durations to fit your workflow.
  • Track progress: Session stats are saved in your browser (localStorage).
  • Fullscreen mode: Minimize distractions by going fullscreen.
