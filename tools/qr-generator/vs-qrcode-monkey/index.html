Why Choose Pure-Flon QR Generator?

When you encode WiFi passwords or personal contact information into QR codes, privacy matters. Pure-Flon generates everything client-side — your WiFi password or contact details never touch a server.

No ads, no pop-ups, no cookie consent banners. Open the page, generate your QR code, download it. That is the entire workflow.

The QR code is generated using the open-source qrcode.js library directly in your browser. You can verify this by checking the page source — no API calls are made.

Need a quick QR code during a presentation or while setting up a restaurant menu? Pure-Flon loads in under a second with no dependencies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does QR Code Monkey require an account?

No account is required for basic QR code generation. However, QR Code Monkey offers premium features (dynamic QR codes, tracking, analytics) that require sign-up. Pure-Flon QR Generator is fully featured without any account or sign-up.

Can Pure-Flon QR Generator add a logo to the QR code?

Logo embedding is not yet available. QR Code Monkey offers this feature. For most use cases (URLs, WiFi, vCards), a standard QR code without a logo works perfectly and scans more reliably across all devices.

Which tool generates higher quality QR codes?

Both tools generate fully functional QR codes that scan reliably. Pure-Flon offers SVG export for infinitely scalable, print-ready codes. The key difference is privacy: Pure-Flon generates entirely in your browser while QR Code Monkey processes on their servers.
