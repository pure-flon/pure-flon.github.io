Why Choose Pure-Flon Color Palette Generator?

CSS custom properties (variables) export is available with one click. Copy and paste directly into your stylesheet. Coolors requires a premium subscription for some export formats.

No account creation, no saved passwords, no email verification. Open the tool, pick your seed color, export your palette. The entire flow takes under 10 seconds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Coolors free?

Coolors has a free tier with ads and limited features. Full functionality (PDF export, unlimited saved palettes, collaboration, some export formats) requires a Pro subscription at approximately $3 per month. Pure-Flon Color Palette Generator is completely free with no feature restrictions.

Can Pure-Flon export Tailwind CSS colors?

Yes. Pure-Flon's Color Palette Generator has a built-in Tailwind export button that outputs a ready-to-paste Tailwind config object with all five palette colors. This feature is not natively available in Coolors.

Which tool is better for professional designers?

Coolors offers more advanced features like palette exploration from images, gradient generation, and collaboration tools. However, for the core workflow of generating a harmonious palette and exporting to CSS or Tailwind, Pure-Flon is faster and freer. Many developers use both — Coolors for exploration and Pure-Flon for quick CSS-ready exports.
