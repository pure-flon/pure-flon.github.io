Frequently asked questions

What data sources does it support?

Google Sheets, Excel, CSV uploads, REST APIs, webhooks, and direct ERP connectors (SAP, NetSuite, Odoo). Shopify and HubSpot integrations are in beta. If you need a custom source, contact us.

How is "anomaly" defined? Can I customize it?

Yes. The AI learns a baseline from 30–90 days of your history. You can also set hard thresholds manually (e.g., "alert if order volume drops > 30% vs 7-day MA"). Both modes can run in parallel.

Is my operations data secure?

Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We do not share or sell your data. Enterprise plans include single-tenant deployment and custom data residency options.

How long does setup take?

Most customers are connected and receiving their first alerts within 5–15 minutes. The AI baseline training completes within 24 hours of connecting your first data source.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled any time from your dashboard — no penalty, no questions. You keep access until the end of the current billing period.
