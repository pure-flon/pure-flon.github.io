PTFE Tube Grades

Available in multiple grades to match your application requirements

Standard Grade

General industrial use. Extruded virgin PTFE, consistent dimensions, economical pricing for high-volume orders.

High Purity Grade

Ultra-pure for semiconductor and laboratory. Low extractables, particle-free surface. CoA provided per batch.

Food/Pharma Grade

FDA 21 CFR + NSF/ANSI 51 compliant. For food contact, beverage, and pharmaceutical processing applications.

Convoluted / Corrugated

Maximum flexibility with PTFE chemical resistance. For tight routing, vibration isolation, multi-axis movement.
