Chemical Resistance and Fit

PFA is usually selected when the buyer wants the purity language of a premium fluoropolymer line without giving up visibility during process monitoring.

HF and sulfuric acid Strong chemical resistance for aggressive semiconductor and chemical process media.
Hydrochloric and nitric acid Fits buyers who need corrosion resistance but also a cleaner regulated-process story.
Hydrogen peroxide Suitable for oxidizing media when purity, extractables, and handling consistency matter.
IPA, acetone, and MEK Useful for solvent service where process teams still want a high-clarity tube path.
UPW and DI water Good fit for clean utility distribution where purity claims must remain credible.
Move to PTFE if needed Switch when maximum temperature or broader industrial media becomes the stronger buying criterion.
