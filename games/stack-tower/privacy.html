1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Stack Tower, a mobile game published by pure-flon (Seoul, South Korea) on the Google Play Store and other distribution channels. It describes how we collect, use, and disclose information when you install or play the game.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected

When you use Stack Tower, the following is automatically collected for analytics and crash reporting:

  • Device model, OS version, language, country, time zone
  • App version, install source, session timestamps and duration
  • Game events (level completed, score, retries, IAP purchases)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID) — only if you consent
  • IP address (temporarily, for anti-fraud and regional compliance)

2.2 You Do NOT Provide

Stack Tower does not require account creation. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address, or any personally identifiable information directly from you.

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain the game and its features
  • Measure retention, engagement, and monetization performance
  • Diagnose crashes and performance issues
  • Serve relevant advertising (if you have consented)
  • Detect fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

Stack Tower integrates the following third-party SDKs, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config) — policy
  • Google Play Services (authentication, in-app purchases) — policy
  • AppLovin MAX (ad mediation, advertising) — policy
  • AppsFlyer (attribution, install tracking) — policy
  • Unity Analytics (game telemetry) — policy

These services may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and in-game events to provide their services.

5. Advertising

Stack Tower shows rewarded video ads (optional) and interstitial ads (after level 5, every 3 levels). Ads are served through AppLovin MAX mediation, which may show ads from Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, and other networks.

You can disable personalized ads in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

You can also remove all ads permanently via the one-time in-app purchase "Remove Ads" ($2.99 USD).

6. Data Retention

  • Firebase Analytics events: up to 14 months (Google default)
  • Crash reports: 90 days
  • IAP purchase records: retained as long as required by tax law and Google Play Terms
  • Local device data (save files, settings): until you uninstall the app

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the European Union, United Kingdom, or California, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email contact@pure-flon.com with the subject line "GDPR Request" or "CCPA Request" and include your device's advertising ID (found in device Settings → Google → Ads).

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Stack Tower is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly. Parents who believe their child has provided personal information to us can contact contact@pure-flon.com to request deletion.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Material changes will be announced in-app or on this page at least 30 days before they take effect.
