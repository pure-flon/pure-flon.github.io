Coordination desk

Follow-up without an account portal.

This page replaces the old authenticated dashboard. PURE-FLON currently routes quote updates, order follow-up, sample coordination, and document requests through direct email coordination and the public RFQ flow.

Send RFQ or update Email contact@pure-flon.com

Best use cases

  • follow up on an already submitted quote
  • request a datasheet, compliance file, or sample note
  • send revised drawings, quantities, or delivery targets
  • route technical questions to the support lane

What happens now

1

Send the RFQ or update

Use the RFQ form for new requests, or email the same inbox with your quote number, tubing family, size range, and target quantity for existing follow-up.

2

Receive technical routing

The team reviews product fit, requested documents, and any engineering questions directly in the coordination thread instead of moving you into a separate login flow.

3

Continue through the same contact lane

Revisions, sample requests, shipping notes, and order clarification stay attached to the same thread so nothing is blocked by missing account access.
